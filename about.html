About AETHELL

Aethell was created to revolutionize modern home decor with mirrors designed to match personal aesthetics while maintaining a premium feel.

Inspired by internet culture, music visuals and contemporary interiors, each mirror becomes more than decoration — it becomes a centerpiece.

The brand started from a simple idea: finding something that matched a room's aesthetic while still feeling luxurious.
