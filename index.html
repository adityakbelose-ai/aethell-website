THE STORY

It started from a simple problem. I wanted something for my room that looked cool, minimal and premium but most mirrors available felt basic or outdated.

So Aethell was born. A brand focused on modern, Gen-Z inspired mirror designs that elevate your space while reflecting your personality.

Each mirror is designed to feel like more than just decor—it becomes a statement piece in your room. We also offer custom designs so you can create something that perfectly fits your aesthetic and vision.

Aethell isn't just about mirrors - it's about creating a space that feels uniquely yours.

REFUNDS & RETURNS

At Aethell, each mirror is treated as a piece of art. Because of the delicate and bespoke nature of our products:

Returns are not possible once the order is placed and delivered.
