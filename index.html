About AETHELL

Aethell was created to bring a new perspective to modern home decor. The brand focuses on mirrors that do more than reflect a room — they shape the aesthetic of the space itself.

Inspired by music culture, internet design and modern interiors, each mirror blends artistic influence with premium craftsmanship.

The idea began from a simple goal: finding a mirror that matched a personal aesthetic without compromising on quality. Aethell exists to create pieces that feel expressive, modern and luxurious at the same time.
