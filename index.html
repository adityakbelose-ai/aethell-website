About AETHELL

It started from a simple problem. I wanted something for my room that looked cool, minimal and premium but most mirrors available felt basic or outdated. So Aethell was born. A brand focused on modern, Gen-Z inspired mirror designs that elevate your space while reflecting your personality. Each mirror is designed to feel like more than just decor. It becomes a statement piece in your room. We also offer custom mirror designs so you can create something that perfectly fits your aesthetic and vision. Aethell isn't just about mirrors - it's about creating a space that feels uniquely yours.
