About AETHELL

AETHELL began from a simple frustration. Mirrors rarely felt expressive enough for modern spaces.

The idea was simple: create mirrors that feel like art objects instead of plain furniture.

Each design blends internet culture, nostalgia, and minimal luxury aesthetics.
